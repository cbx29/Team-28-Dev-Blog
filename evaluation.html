Solution Summary

Multiplayer Interactions demonstrated with an Open Source PC Sports Game Engine

In summary, our team has successfully expanded the MotionInput project to incorporate multiplayer functionality, achieving our goals of providing users with a truly immersive hands-free device interaction experience with their friends. This breakthrough not only allows individuals with disabilities to participate in group exercises and engage in multiplayer gaming but also broadens the range of games that can be played with MotionInput.

Our team is excited to have accomplished this major milestone and to have opened up new opportunities for users to engage with each other in a whole new way. With this latest advancement, the possibilities for MotionInput are endless, and we look forward to continuing to innovate and push the boundaries of what is possible in the realm of hands-free device interaction.
